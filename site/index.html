Workwright

Humans above the loop, not in it.

Workwright is a crit system for building things with AI. A wright does the work and defends its choices — why this form and not another. You judge the defense, not just the output. Your judgment becomes the taste guide. Over time, the work gets better because the system learns what you actually value, not what you say you value.

What Is This

The Two Questions Why are we making this? How does it solve it elegantly?
Defense, Not Just Diffs Every change comes with a conceptual defense. Why this form and not another?
Crit, Not Review Review asks "does this look okay." Crit asks "should this exist in this form."
Taste as Gradient Humans don't review every diff. They shape what "good" means through repeated judgment.
One Protocol Wrights, humans, teams — same loop. Task, work, defense, crit, taste signal.
